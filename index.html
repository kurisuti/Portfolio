Nice to meet you.

My name is Christie, I'm a UX Designer from Los Angeles.

I enjoy creating thoughtful human experiences.

This is my Design Process.

Strategy

I ask a lot of questions, but I'm not trying to be annoying. I ask questions to make sure I am aligned with your expectations for a project. This stage includes doing market research and competitive analysis.

Structure

Who doesn't like a great foundation? At this stage, I analyze data received from my research to prioritize user stories that would determine the information hierarchy of the project. Here is where the MVP is created.

Visual Design

The best designs are the ones that evoke feelings. I convert wireframes into their final design look. In order to provide teh best experience of the product, I create a dynamic prototype that displays the final results before having it sent to development.
