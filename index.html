Me in a Nutshell.

Hey there, my name is Christie and I am a designer. Art has always been a part of my life. I have a bachelors in Fine Arts and a minor in Digital Arts. After graduating, I was a graphic designer for six years. Despite the experience gained in my workplaces, I’ve always encouraged myself to continue learning. I enrolled myself in a Designer Track program with Bloc, where I spent over 800 hours learning best design practices by applying UX Research, Visual Design, and Front-end Development to my projects.
When I’m not working, you can find me at Disneyland eating a corndog.

My Design Process.

Strategy

I ask a lot of questions, but I’m not trying to be annoying. I ask questions to make sure that I am aligned with your expectations for a project. This stage includes doing market research and competitive analysis.

Structure

Who doesn’t like a great foundation? At this stage, I analyze data received from my research to prioritize user stories that would determine the information heirarchy of the project. Here is where the MVP is created.

Visual Design

The best designs are the ones that evoke feelings. I convert wireframes into their final design look. In order to provide the best experience of the product, I create a dynamic prototype that displays the final results before having it sent to development.
